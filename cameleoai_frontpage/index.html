Network Analysis Capability

Net Analyzer is a forensic-grade PCAP and flow analysis platform built for speed, trust, and explainability—combining high-performance ingestion (PCAP/CSV/Parquet), advanced telemetry (JA3/TLS fingerprinting, entropy, drift detection), and a hybrid neuro-symbolic engine that fuses heuristic sensors with Bayesian reasoning to produce clear, auditable threat conclusions and interactive what-if simulations. Powered by a Rust backend, it delivers deterministic, memory-safe processing with low CPU overhead, enabling fast analysis on large captures while reducing crash risk and supply-chain exposure—critical for SOC and CISO environments. Every artifact is protected with SHA-256 chain-of-custody, privacy redaction (“Masked Mode”), and backend-verified results, so teams can confidently investigate incidents, generate compliance-ready reports, and share findings without compromising integrity or sensitive data.
